Virus?

Today the world faces a threat from something we cannot see. Something that does not think. It’s not alive. It does not discriminate and had no vendetta against you. It is just doing its job; to make copies of itself. It does not make a strategy. You do. And you have failed. Now just change the part "it does not think". Would you be concerned?
