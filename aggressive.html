Aggressive

Aggression is an inner trait. It is the clawing feeling that makes you want to get up and go. It is the feeling that dissects failures and makes you get up early and spend the late nights honing your craft.

It is directed outwards, not at others, but at your actions. It is the source of the initiative that you take. It is proactive, but channeled and intense.

Be aggressive.
